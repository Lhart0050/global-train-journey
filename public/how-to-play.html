Control Your Joystick

How to Control the Joystick

To control the joystick, follow these steps:

  • Hold the joystick firmly with both hands.
  • Move the joystick forward to move the train forward.
  • Move it backward to reverse the train.
Handling Questions

What if You Cannot Answer?

If you cannot answer a question, don't worry! You have the following options:

  • Use a hint provided to guide you towards the correct answer.
  • Ask for help from a teacher or a fellow player.
  • Attempt the question again after some time.
Earn Points

How to Get Points

You can earn points by:

  • Answering questions correctly.
  • Completing modules efficiently.
  • Participating in bonus challenges.
  • Helping other players when they need assistance.
Sensor Detection

How Can Sensors Detect?

Our sensors ensure the train stops precisely in front of each statue by:

  • Using infrared sensors to detect the position of the train.
  • Calibrating the stopping distance to align with the statue's location.
  • Providing real-time feedback to adjust the train's speed.
